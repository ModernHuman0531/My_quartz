Subject: FrontierLab Research Internship Inquiry – Yu-Shyang Wei (NYCU)

Dear Professor Yoshikawa,

My name is Yu-Shyang Wei, a senior Mechanical Engineering student at National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University (NYCU), Taiwan. I have recently been nominated for the FrontierLab exchange program and would like to inquire about the possibility of joining your laboratory as a visiting research student for one year starting from [September, 2026] to [September, 2027].

I am particularly interested in your research on autonomous intelligent systems based on robot learning, especially the integration of machine learning and robotics for real-world embodied intelligence. Your work on imitation learning and large language model-based robot learning for object manipulation and intention understanding is especially inspiring to me.

My background has focused on reinforcement learning and robotic control. I have implemented Q-learning and DQN for autonomous navigation tasks and worked on manipulation and perception-control integration using ROS and real robotic platforms. Through these experiences, I became increasingly interested in how learning-based methods can be extended from low-level control to higher-level cognitive functions such as task understanding and human-robot collaboration.

I am especially motivated to explore how reinforcement learning and imitation learning can be combined with language-guided representations to enable more adaptive and context-aware robotic behavior.

I would be honored to contribute to your research on autonomous intelligent systems and embodied robot learning. I have attached my CV for your reference and would sincerely appreciate the opportunity to further discuss potential research directions in your laboratory.

Thank you very much for your time and consideration.
