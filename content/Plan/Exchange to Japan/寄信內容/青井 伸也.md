My name is Yu-Shyang Wei, a senior Mechanical Engineering student at National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University (NYCU), Taiwan. For legged or humanoid robots, I am especially interested in exploring how reinforcement learning can complement dynamical modeling and stability analysis—such as improving adaptability to uneven terrain while preserving the stability guaranteed by analytical control design.

I would be honored to contribute to your laboratory’s work on locomotion modeling and robotic implementation. I have attached my CV for your reference and would sincerely appreciate the opportunity to discuss possible research directions under your supervision.
